Care for me

Like any older vehicle, some repairs could be done, if you were so inclined. We never did as they are cosmetic, and never bothered us. In the interests of honesty, I've listed everything I can think of that you should consider before buying our van. We've also dropped our asking price in line with cost to fix these issues.

  • Rust Thankfully our van doesn't have much. There is some surface rust here and there, and two slightly more serious patches we've maintained with rust stop.
  • The dent Oh dear, weeks after buying Archie we managed to dent two of the panels on a post.
  • Air conditioning Doesn't work. Not sure why, but it just never worked.
  • Fog lights More decorative than functional.
  • Also, the sticky on the bottom of the cup-holder isn't sticky.
